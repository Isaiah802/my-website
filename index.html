The Light You Give Test Test 1 2

In every dawn, you are the light, That pushes shadows far from sight. When skies grow dark, and hopes grow thin, You lift me up, you help me win.

Your steady hands, your caring heart, A quiet strength, a work of art. No mountain seems too tall to climb, When you are standing by my side.

Through every storm, through every tear, Your love remains, forever near. A guiding star, a constant grace, That lights the path I dare to trace.

And every time I feel I fall, I hear your voice, your gentle call: "Keep going, love, you're strong, you'll see," Because you always believe in me.
"You are the bestest most beautiful mother ever."
