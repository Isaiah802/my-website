Happy Birthday Naomi

A cousin so bright, with a heart full of cheer,
A mom who shines, year after year.
Short in stature, but big in grace,
You light up the world with your smile on your face.

From dancing on tables at Wendy's with flair,
To babysitting us with love and care.
You've always been there, a joy and a friend,
With laughter and kindness that never end.

Today’s your day, so let’s make it grand,
Enjoy the hamster—your new little friend!
You’ve filled our hearts, and you always will,
With your happy spirit and endless goodwill.

So here’s to you, Naomi, on your special day,
May joy and love come your way!
