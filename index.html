The Light You Give

Elijah, with his love for Poe,
Can talk for hours, and you’ll never know,
If he’s deep in thought or just cracking a joke,
Either way, you’ll always get hope.

He’s the guy who’s there when you’re in pain,
With a kind heart and a helping brain.
And when he plays music, it’s like magic too,
You’ll want to hear more — he’s that good, it’s true!

He’s into Vikings and the old-school stuff,
But throw in a laugh, and he’s never tough.
He’s got a love for learning, always seeking more,
And no matter what, he’s never a bore.

For family, for church, for friends, he’s there,
A cousin, an uncle — someone who cares.
So here’s to Elijah, let’s give him a cheer,
The best cousin and uncle — year after year!
